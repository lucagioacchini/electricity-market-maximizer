How can we help you?

With this application a company operating in the Italian electricity market can maximize its profits. You got it right! Maximize your profits. Whether you’re a small company or a market giant, it doesn’t matter, this platform will advise you on how to manage your resources and your profits will skyrocket.


It is proposed a data-based predictive platform able to advise the best strategy to the operator in terms of quantity and prices to bid in the markets that articulate the most generic spot markets. This is an achievable result through a clever collection of data, forecasting the markets bids of the next day by exploiting Auto Regressive Integrated Moving Average and an optimization algorithm that maximizes operators' profits.
